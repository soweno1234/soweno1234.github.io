Fabian Borre

Principal Interior Designer | Member of Master Builder Association (MBA)

Fabian Borre is the principal and lead interior designer on our team, specializing in the creation of functional, beautiful, simple, open spaces. Fabian's interior design branding is "Transformation of Function + Style." Fabian sees himself as a simplifier who designs beautiful, functional spaces. Fabian's design philosophy is a combination of style and order. As a modern designer, he believes he has a combination of right- and left-brain thinking. Fabian considers that beautiful spaces are the harmonious integration of function and design. Fabian delights in working with a variety of materials. He advocates for opening up and exposing spaces to emphasize their beauty and create a functional and luxurious lifestyle for his clients.

Fabian started designing trade show exhibits for fashion shows in Las Vegas and New York. His true passion remains in residential and custom commercial projects. Fabian lives in Seattle surrounded with his family and friends. Fabian is a true animal lover. He lives with his three dogs and lately he rescued an abandoned and adorable kitty from his neighborhood. Fabian is passionate about speaking for animal rights and he uses his activist voice to eradicate animal suffering.



Meg is a one-of-a-kind talented interior designer with over thirty years of experience designing commercial and residential projects. Meg has worked on projects ranging from hospitality, office spaces, multi-unit residential projects, and private residences. Meg creatively translates intricate design concepts from the mind's eye to a visual reality. Using three-dimensional imagery and two-dimensional construction drawings, Meg creates all the digital visual aids to provide clients with a design preview. These digital previews showcase the transformation of each space prior to design implementation. Once the design is approved, Meg develops all technical and construction drawings to allow for the execution of the design.

Her love of architecture, from modern to classic, gives her a unique perspective that she brings to our clients. Meg loves the beach and has a tremendous passion for art and photography. In her free time, Meg can be found at the beach, enjoying the sun and the sand. She has a tremendous passion for art and photography and often incorporates elements of both into her designs.

Hannah Narrow

Project Designer, Stylist | LEED Certified | Interior Design Association (IIDA)

Hannah Narrow is a Project Designer with seven years of experience. Hannah received her Bachelor of Applied Arts in Interior Design locally from Bellevue College and has worked on both residential and commercial projects. Hannah’s broad range of project experience allows her to wear many hats while being creative and designing unique spaces for each client’s needs. With a background in both architecture and interior design, Hannah has a unique perspective on the design process and is able to create spaces that are both functional and visually stunning.

Hannah’s passion for materiality and attention to detail give her an eye for quality craftsmanship and she believes that materiality and furnishings are integral parts to creating a cohesive design. Hannah approaches design from both a conceptual and analytical point of view, creating a thoughtful and unique space for each client. Hannah enjoys spending her free time with her family and friends and traveling around the Pacific Northwest. She is also passionate about sustainable design and is always looking for ways to incorporate green building practices into her projects.
