Bio

Soren is the founder and CEO of WaterH, a rapidly growing water bottle company that is changing the way people think about hydration. Soren's passion for sustainability and his desire to create a positive impact on the environment inspired him to launch WaterH in 2015. Born and raised in Washington, Soren was always conscious of the state's ongoing water crisis and the need to conserve water. He began researching ways to help people reduce their environmental impact and discovered that a major source of waste was single-use plastic water bottles. He saw an opportunity to create a more sustainable alternative that was both functional and stylish. Soren worked tirelessly to develop a high-quality, reusable water bottle that was easy to use and made from environmentally friendly materials. He collaborated with a team of designers and engineers to create a sleek and durable bottle that was both BPA-free and recyclable.

Our Mission

Save the world from lukewarm


Our mission at WaterH is to provide high-quality, eco-friendly water bottles that empower people to stay hydrated and reduce their impact on the environment. We strive to design products that are not only functional and stylish but also sustainable and durable, so that our customers can enjoy them for years to come. By promoting a healthy lifestyle and encouraging the use of reusable water bottles, we hope to contribute to a more sustainable future for our planet.

Our Values

The backbone of our brand is, and always has been, a commitment to a set of core values that define who we are and how we design our water bottles.


Our values are rooted in the belief that we have a responsibility to protect the environment and encourage people to make positive choices for themselves and the planet. We prioritize quality and durability in our products, ensuring that they are long-lasting and reliable. We also believe in transparency and ethical business practices, treating our customers, partners, and employees with respect and integrity. As a company, we strive to make a positive impact on society and the environment, and our values are at the heart of everything we do.
